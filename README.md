## Project Overview
_This project focuses on analyzing mobile sales performance across various dimensions, including revenue, units sold, customer gender, payment methods, monthly trends, and geographical locations. The aim of the analysis is to uncover sales patterns, customer behaviors, and revenue distribution in order to support data-driven decision-making and business growth strategies.
